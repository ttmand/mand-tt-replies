There are two types of bans:

- Temporary bans

This is where you cannot send chat messages, make forum threads, or reply to threads for 12 hours. The ban will end 12 hours after it was given.

- Permanent bans

If you get a "permanent ban", you will be unable to log into your account at all. Your account is basically blocked. This will last until a moderator unbans you. However, as the name suggests, an unban will typically not happen.
